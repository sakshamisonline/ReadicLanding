Privacy Policy

Effective date: [DATE BEFORE LAUNCH]  ·  Last updated: [DATE BEFORE LAUNCH]

We built Readic to help you learn — not to profit from your personal data. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what rights you have over it.

1. Who we are

Readic ("Readic," "we," "our," or "us") is a vocabulary learning platform operated by Saksham, a sole proprietorship based in Kurukshetra, Haryana, India. Our platform is accessible at readic.in and through our Chrome browser extension.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@readic.in

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the Readic service.

2.1 Information you give us directly

Data Why we collect it
Name and email address To create and manage your account
Password (we cannot read it) To authenticate you securely
Native language preference To provide translations in your language

2.2 Information collected automatically through your use

Data Why we collect it
Words you look up To show your search history and log entries to your library
Words you save to your library To build and maintain your personal word collection
The sentence surrounding a saved word (context sentence) To help you remember where you first encountered the word
The URL of the page where you saved a word To show the source article/page in your library
Practice and mastery data (which words you got right or wrong) To track your learning progress
Reading time data (only if you enable the reading tracker feature) To show your reading habits and progress. Collected only with your explicit consent per website — you choose which sites count.

2.3 Payment information

When you subscribe to a paid plan, payments are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited. Readic does not store your card number, CVV, or banking details. We only receive a confirmation of successful payment and a subscription ID from Razorpay. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions: razorpay.com/privacy

2.4 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect the full text of pages you visit
  • We do not track your browsing activity on websites where our extension is inactive
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the service — looking up words, saving to library, translation, practice modes
  • To personalize your experience — translations in your native language, showing your history, calculating mastery levels
  • To manage your subscription — tracking plan limits, processing payments, sending receipts
  • To communicate with you — account-related emails (receipts, password resets, important service updates). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in.
  • To improve Readic — understanding which features are used most, identifying bugs

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers, and only to the extent necessary to operate Readic:

Third party What is shared Why
Cloud database provider Account and word/library data (encrypted at rest) To securely store your account and app data
Razorpay Name, email, subscription plan Payment processing
Dictionary data provider The word you are looking up only — no personal identity To fetch definitions
Translation service provider The word or phrase to be translated — no personal identity To provide translations
AI language processing provider Text you submit for AI-based features (e.g., word explanations, described feelings, conversation practice) — no more account information than needed to return the response To power AI-based learning features (Useful and Effective plans)
Cloud hosting providers Website and API traffic To run our website and backend servers

We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Your rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

As a user in India, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — You can request a summary of what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — You can update your name, email, and language preference from your Settings page at any time
  • Right to erasure — You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of such a request. To request deletion, email privacy@readic.in with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."
  • Right to withdraw consent — You can disable the reading tracker or any opt-in feature from your Settings page at any time
  • Right to grievance redressal — If you believe your data rights have been violated, you may contact our grievance officer at privacy@readic.in. We will respond within 72 hours.

6. Data security

  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard one-way hashing — we never store or can view your actual password
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Account sessions use secure, expiring authentication tokens
  • Access to our systems is restricted and requires authentication

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@readic.in.

7. Data retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting compliance under Indian law
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., "how many users looked up word X") may be retained indefinitely as they contain no personal information

8. Children's privacy

Readic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@readic.in and we will delete the account promptly.

9. Cookies and local storage

Readic uses localStorage in your browser to store your login session token (JWT) so you remain logged in between visits. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. The browser extension uses chrome.storage.sync to store your authentication token locally in Chrome — this data does not leave your device except to communicate with Readic's servers.

10. International users

Readic is operated from India. If you access Readic from outside India, please be aware that your data will be transferred to and processed in India. By using Readic, you consent to this transfer. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy regardless of where it is processed.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, notify you by email or via an in-app notification. Continued use of Readic after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
