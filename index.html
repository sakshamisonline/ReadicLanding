How it works

Three steps to never forget a word

Readic fits seamlessly into your reading habit — no switching tabs, no breaking your flow.

01
Select any word

Highlight any single word on any website. A tooltip appears instantly above it with the definition, easy pronunciation, and an audio button.

02
Translate & understand

On the Useful and Effective plans, one click translates your saved words into your native language — Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, or more. No new tab. No interruption.

03
Save & master it

Hit save and the word goes straight into your personal library with its context, source page, and translation. Then practise with flashcards, fill-in-the-blank, or dictation.
