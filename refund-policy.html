Refund Policy

Effective date: [DATE BEFORE LAUNCH]

We want you to feel confident subscribing to Readic. This policy explains exactly when you can get a refund and how to request one — no complicated process.

Monthly subscriptions

Monthly plans (Useful $1.99/month, Effective $4.99/month) are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started. You can cancel at any time from Settings to prevent future charges. You retain access until the end of the paid period.

Exceptions — always considered

We will always consider refund requests for:

  • Accidental double charge — if you were charged twice for the same period
  • Technical failure — if a technical issue on our side prevented you from accessing paid features for a significant portion of your billing period
  • Unauthorized charge — if you believe your account was charged without your authorization

How to request a refund

Email us at support@readic.in with:

  • Subject line: "Refund Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • The reason for your request

We will respond within 48 hours. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and returned to your original payment method via Razorpay.

Cancellation vs refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current period. If you want a refund for the current period, you must request it separately using the process above.
